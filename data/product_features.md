# Focusly — Product Features Reference

## Core Features (Required for Activation)
- **Focus Timer**: Pomodoro-style timer (25min work / 5min break)
- **Task Manager**: Create, prioritise, and complete daily tasks
- **Study Planner**: Weekly study schedule builder

## Advanced Features (Unlock after activation)
- **Deep Work Mode**: 90-minute uninterrupted focus sessions with ambient sound
- **Progress Analytics**: Weekly productivity score and streak tracking
- **Study Groups**: Invite friends to co-study sessions
- **AI Study Coach**: Personalised study tips based on your patterns
- **Calendar Sync**: Connect Google Calendar to auto-block study time

## Activation Milestone Definition
A user is considered "activated" when they:
1. Complete their first Focus Timer session (25 min)
2. Add at least 3 tasks to Task Manager
3. Build their first weekly Study Plan

## Engagement Score Calculation
- Daily login: +5 points
- Focus Timer completion: +10 points
- Task completed: +3 points
- Study group joined: +15 points
- Streak maintained (7 days): +20 points
- Score decays: -2 points per inactive day
