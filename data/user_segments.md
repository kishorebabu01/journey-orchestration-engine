# Focusly — User Segment Definitions

## Segment 1: High-Intent Students
- Source: Google Ads (study productivity keywords)
- Behaviour: Sign up with a specific goal ("A-levels in 3 months")
- Activation rate: 68%
- Best channel: Email (open rate 34%)
- Message style: Achievement-focused, milestone-driven

## Segment 2: Social Referrals
- Source: Friend referral or study group invite
- Behaviour: Browse features, compare with friends
- Activation rate: 71%
- Best channel: Push notification (CTR 18%)
- Message style: Social proof, community-focused

## Segment 3: Organic Discovery
- Source: App store or blog post
- Behaviour: Explore slowly, low urgency
- Activation rate: 41%
- Best channel: In-app tooltip (engagement rate 22%)
- Message style: Curiosity-driven, feature discovery

## Segment 4: Paid Social (Meta/TikTok)
- Source: Instagram or TikTok ad
- Behaviour: Quick signup, easily distracted
- Activation rate: 29%
- Best channel: Push notification within 2 hours
- Message style: Urgency, FOMO, social proof

## Churn Risk Indicators
- No session for 5+ days: Early warning
- No session for 7+ days: Churn risk confirmed
- Engagement score below 20: Passive user
- Last feature used > 14 days ago: High churn risk
