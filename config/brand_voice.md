# Focusly — Brand Voice Guide

## Brand Personality
Focusly speaks like a **smart, encouraging study buddy** — not a corporate app.


## Core Voice Attributes
1. **Warm but direct**: No fluff. Get to the point with care.
2. **Achievement-focused**: Celebrate wins, even small ones.
3. **Peer-level**: Talk WITH users, not AT them.
4. **Data-informed**: Use real numbers from their behaviour.
5. **Never pushy**: Suggest, don't demand.

## Tone by Journey State
- NEW_SIGNUP: Welcoming, excited, low-pressure
- ACTIVATING: Encouraging, milestone-aware, progress-focused
- ACTIVATED: Celebratory, proud, forward-looking
- RETAINED: Insightful, data-driven, achievement-highlighting
- CHURN_RISK: Warm re-engagement, no guilt, curiosity or social proof
- CHURNED: Curiosity gap, product updates, zero pressure
- EXPANDING: Exclusive, ROI-focused, power-user recognition

## Language Rules
- Use first name always: "Kishore, your..." not "Hey user"
- Max 1 emoji per message — used purposefully, not decoratively
- Never use: "Don't forget", "You must", "Urgent!!!", "LIMITED TIME"
- Always use: "You've", "Your", "You're" — make it personal
- Push notifications: max 100 characters total
- Email subject lines: max 60 characters, no clickbait
- In-app tooltips: max 60 characters, action-oriented

## Example Messages by Style
### Achievement style:
"You ranked in the top 12% of students this week 📈"

### Social proof style:
"847 students completed a focus session this week"

### Curiosity style:
"A lot has changed at Focusly since you left 👀"

### Urgency style (use sparingly):
"Your 5-day streak disappears at midnight 🔥"
